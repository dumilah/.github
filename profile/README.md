[DUMILAH](https://fib.unud.ac.id/pages/digital-humanities) is a Digital Humanities initiative at the Faculty of Humanities, Udayana University, Bali, Indonesia.

The prequel of DUMILAH was a series of research talks (the so-called [*Fakultas Ilmu Budaya* [FIB] *DigiTalk*](https://www.cirhss.org/fib-digitalk/)). 

Upon its formal establishment on 23 April 2026, DUMILAH is supported by a registry of research outcomes on [Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/communities/dumilah-fib-unud/records?q=&l=list&p=1&s=10) (see the [Curation policy](https://zenodo.org/communities/dumilah-fib-unud/curation-policy) before submitting).
